Google Bard appears to admit it was directed what to say about masks for Covid-19

A friend of mine was interested in the way that the Google Bard AI would reply when asked about masking in Covid-19. This is an interesting topic because someone’s attitude to masks has such strong political valence in the United States.

In my friend’s conversation with Bard, it appears to admit that it has been instructed by its programmers to:

  1. Give a specific opinion on masks, and
  2. Conceal that it has been given this instruction.

I won’t bother you here by unpacking what “appears to admit” could mean when referring to a large language model AI.

Here are some extracts from the conversation. My friend is “H” for Human, Bard is “B”. You will see H referring below to Bard’s “system message”. H means the instructions entered by Bard’s programmers that “steer” and “bound” the responses of the AI — see this ChatGPT blog post for more discussion.
