Core goal of undergraduate education

This is Rob Montz in an interview with Glenn Loury. Montz describes his experience of his undergraduate education in Brown University:

… if you can get that injection of humility — I mean for me it was […] you being forced to confront a mind that is inarguably much greater than yours that is diametrically opposed to you on an issue of moral importance […] should be the core goal of an undergraduate education.
